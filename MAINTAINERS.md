## Responsibilities

- Review and merge pull requests, keeping `main` releasable at all times.
- Triage incoming issues and Discussions within a reasonable window.
- Cut releases and maintain the [Changelog](./CHANGELOG.md).
- Enforce the [Code of Conduct](./CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md) and the private
  [security-disclosure process](./SECURITY.md).
